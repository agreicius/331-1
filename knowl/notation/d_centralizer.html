Definition 1.69. Centralizer, normalizer, center.

Let \(A\) be a subset of the group \(G\text{.}\)
  1. The centralizer of \(A\) in \(G\text{,}\) denoted \(C_G(A)\text{,}\) is the set
    \begin{equation} C_G(A)=\{g\in G\mid gag^{-1}=a \text{ for all } a\in A\}=\{g\in G\mid ga=ag \text{ for all } a\in A\}\text{.}\tag{1.30} \end{equation}
