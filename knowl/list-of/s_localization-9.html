Notation 2.10.7. .

Let \(R\) be an integral domain. Since the localization map \(\lambda\colon R\rightarrow \Frac R\) is injective, we will often identify \(R\) with its image
\begin{align*} \lambda(R) \amp =\{r/1\mid r\in R\}\subseteq \Frac R\text{,} \end{align*}
allowing us to think of \(R\) as a subring of \(\Frac R\text{.}\)
