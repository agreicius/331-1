Corollary 1.19.10. Stabilizers of cycles.

Let \(\sigma=(a_1\ a_2\ \dots a_k)\) be a \(k\)-cycle in \(S_n\text{.}\) Let \(H=\angvec{\sigma}\) and let
\begin{equation*} K=\{\tau\in S_n\mid \tau(a_i)=a_i \text{ for all } 1\leq i\leq k\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
We have
\begin{align*} C_{S_n}(\sigma) \amp =HK \amp \abs{C_{S_{n}}(\sigma)}=k(n-k)!\text{.} \end{align*}
