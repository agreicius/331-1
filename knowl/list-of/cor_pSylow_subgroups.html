Corollary 1.167. \(p\)-Sylow subgroups.

Let \(G\) be a finite group, and let \(p\) be a prime divisor of \(\abs{G}\text{.}\)
  1. Any two \(p\)-Sylow subgroups of \(G\) are conjugate. Equivalently, \(\Syl_p(G)\) forms a single orbit under conjugation.
