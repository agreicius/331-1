Corollary 1.120. Fourth isomorphism theorem: quotients.

Let \(N\) be a normal subgroup of \(G\text{.}\) The subgroups of \(G/N\) are in bijective correspondence with the subgroups of \(G\) containing \(N\text{.}\) Furthermore, this correspondence preserves normality, inclusion, intersection, generated subgroups, etc..
