Corollary 2.98. CRT for unit groups.

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, let \(I_1, I_2, \dots, I_n\) be a collection of pairwise relatively prime ideals of \(R\text{,}\) and let
\begin{align*} I \amp =\bigcap_{k=1}^n I_k=\prod_{k=1}^n I_k\text{.} \end{align*}
The map
\begin{align} \phi\colon (R/I)^* \amp\rightarrow \prod_{k=1}^n (R/I_k)^* \tag{2.62}\\ r+I \amp \mapsto (r+I_1, r+I_2,\dots, r+I_n)\tag{2.63} \end{align}
is an isomorphism of groups.
