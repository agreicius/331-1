Corollary 2.10.10. Mapping property of localizations.

Let \(S\) be a multiplicative subset of the nonzero commutative ring \(R\text{,}\) and let \(\lambda\colon R\rightarrow S^{-1}R\) be the localization map. Given any nonzero ring \(T\text{,}\) the map
\begin{align} \Hom(S^{-1}R, T)\amp \rightarrow \{\psi\in \Hom(R,T)\mid \phi(S)\subseteq T^*\}\tag{2.10.9}\\ \phi \amp \mapsto \psi=\phi\circ \lambda\tag{2.10.10} \end{align}
is a bijection.
