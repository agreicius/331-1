Proposition 2.15.10. Total degree.

Let \(R\) be a nonzero commutative ring, let \(n\) be a positive integer, and let \(f\) and \(g\) be nonzero elements of \(R[x_1,x_2,\dots, x_n]\text{.}\)
  1. \(\deg fg\leq \deg f+\deg g\text{,}\) with equality if \(R\) is an integral domain.
