Theorem 1.151. Class equation of \(S_n\).

Let \(n\) be a positive integer, and let \(\mathcal{P}\) be the set of all partitions of \(n\text{.}\) The class equation of \(S_n\) is
\begin{equation} n!=\sum_{(n_1,n_2,\dots, n_r)\in \mathcal{P}}\dfrac{n!}{n_1!n_2 !\cdots n_r!1^{n_1}2^{n_2}\cdots r^{n_r}}\text{.}\tag{1.75} \end{equation}
