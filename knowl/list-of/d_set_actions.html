Definition 1.86. Group operations on subsets.

Let \(A\) be a subset of the group \(G\text{.}\) Given \(g\in G\text{,}\) the sets \(gA\text{,}\) \(Ag\text{,}\) \(gAg^{-1}\) are defined as follows:
\begin{align} A^{-1} \amp =\{a^{-1}\mid a\in G\}\tag{1.38}\\ gA \amp =\{ga\mid a\in A\}\tag{1.39}\\ Ag \amp =\{ag\mid a\in A\}\tag{1.40}\\ gAg^{-1} \amp =\{gag^{-1}\mid a\in A \}\text{.}\tag{1.41} \end{align}
