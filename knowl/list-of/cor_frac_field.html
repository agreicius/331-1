Corollary 2.10.11. Fraction field mapping property.

Let \(R\) be an integral domain with fraction field \(\Frac R\text{,}\) and let \(\lambda\colon R\rightarrow \Frac R\) be the localization map. Given any field \(K\) and ring homomorphism \(\phi\colon R\rightarrow K\text{,}\) there is a unique injective ring homomorphism \(\phi^*\colon \Frac R\rightarrow K\) satisfying \(\phi=\phi^*\lambda\text{.}\)
