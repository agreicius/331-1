Theorem 2.17.11. The isomorphism theorems for modules.

Let \(M\) and \(N\) be \(R\)-modules.
  1. Let \(M'\) be a submodule of the \(R\)-module \(M\text{,}\) and let \(\pi\colon M\rightarrow M/M'\) be the quotient map. Given any \(\phi\in \Hom_R(M,N)\) satisfying \(M'\subseteq \ker \phi\text{,}\) there is a unique \(\overline{\phi}\in \Hom_R(M/M', N)\) satisfying \(\overline{\phi}=\overline{\phi}\circ \pi\text{:}\) namely, the map \(\overline{\phi}\colon M/M'\rightarrow N\) defined as \(\overline{\phi}(m+M')=\phi(m)\text{.}\)
