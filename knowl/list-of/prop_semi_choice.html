Proposition 1.193. Isomorphic semidirect products.

Let \(N\) and \(H\) be groups, and let \(\phi\colon H\rightarrow \Aut(N)\text{.}\)
  1. If \(\psi\in \Aut(H)\text{,}\) then
    \begin{equation*} N\rtimes_{\phi} H\cong N\rtimes_{\phi'}H\text{,} \end{equation*}
    where \(\phi'=\phi\circ \psi\text{.}\)
