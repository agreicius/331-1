Corollary 2.13.6. Primes of \(\Z[i]\).

Up to associates, the distinct irreducible elements \(\pi\) of \(\Z[i]\) are described below based on the unique prime \(p\) they lie over.
  • Case: \(p=2\).
    Up to associates, \(\pi=(1+i)\) is the unique irreducible element of \(\Z[i]\) lying over \(2\text{.}\)
