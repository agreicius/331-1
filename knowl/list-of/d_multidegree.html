Definition 2.15.6. .

Let \(n\) be a positive integer, and let \(\leq\) be any well ordering on \(\N^n\text{:}\) i.e., \(\N^n\) is totally ordered with respect to \(\leq\text{,}\) and every nonempty subset of \(\N^r\) has a least element with respect to \(\leq\text{.}\)

Given a nonzero polynomial \(f=\sum_{\boldi\in \N^n}a_\boldi \boldx^{\boldi}\text{,}\) let \(\boldi_0\) be the greatest element of the set \(\{\boldi\in \N^n\mid a_\boldi\ne 0\}\text{.}\) We define \(a_{\boldi_{0}}\boldx^{\boldi_0}\) to be the leading term of \(f\text{,}\) \(a_{\boldi_0}\) its leading coefficient, and \(\bold_{i_0}\) to be its multidegree with respect to \(\leq\text{,}\) denoted \(\deg_{\leq} f\) (or just \(\deg f\text{,}\) if the context is clear).
