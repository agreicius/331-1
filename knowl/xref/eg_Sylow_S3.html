Example 1.160. Sylow subgroups of \(S_3\).

We have \(\abs{S_3}=6=2\cdot 3\text{.}\) From the lattice of subgroups of \(S_3\text{,}\) we see immediately that
\begin{align*} \Syl_2(S_3) \amp =\{\angvec{(12)}, \angvec{(13)}, \angvec{(23)}\}\\ \Syl_3(S_3) \amp = \{\angvec{(123)}\} \end{align*}
and thus
\begin{align*} n_2(S_3) \amp = 3 \amp n_3(S_3)\amp =1 \end{align*}
