Theorem 1.187. Structure of finite abelian groups: elementary divisors.

Let \(G\) be a finite abelian group of cardinality \(n> 1\text{,}\) let \(p_1,p_2,\dots, p_r\) be the distinct prime divisors of \(n\text{.}\) Assume \(n\) factors as
\begin{equation*} n=\prod_{i=1}^rp_i^{n_i}\text{.} \end{equation*}
  1. \(G\cong \prod_{i=1}^r P_i\text{,}\) where \(P_i\) is an abelian group of cardinality \(p_i^{n_i}\text{.}\)
